About Egerton University Sports Stores

Egerton University Sports and Games Department prides itself in being able to cater for students recreational and competitive needs.
We offer 22 disciplines ranging from ball games, racket games, board games, swimming, athletics and martial art.
The games include:
  • football
  • basketball
  • Hockey
  • Volleyball
  • Tennis
  • Table Tennis
  • Netball
