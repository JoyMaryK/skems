FAQs

How do I book an item ?

1. Go to the top section of this window


2. Click on home


3. Select the item you would like to book


4. Enter the date you want to book it


5. Click book


How do I get the booked item?

1. Go to the pavilion


2. Show your student id card


3. Enter your account password when prompted


4. You can now go with your item


How do I return an item?

1. Go to the pavilion with the item


2. Show your student id card


3. The quality of the item will be assesed


4. Enter your account password when prompted


5. You can now return the item


How do I logout?

On the top-right of the screen, click on the logout button
