FAQs

How do I issue an item to a student?

1. Go to the top section of this window


2. Click on booked


3. Type the student's reg. no. in the search bar


4. When the student's details appear, click on the issue item button


5. Let the student verify themselves by entering their password


6. Issue the item to the student


How do I mark an item as returned?

1. Go to the top section of this window


2. Click on issued


3. Type the student's reg. no. in the search bar


4. When the student's details appear, click on the returned button


5. Let the student verify themselves by entering their password


6. Retrieve the item from the student


How do I add a new item?

1. Go to the top section of this window


2. Click on issued


3. In the new window, click on add item


4. When the pop-up appears, enter the item's details


5. Click save to save the item to the list


How do I get the list of defaulters?

1. Go to the top section of this window


2. Click on issued


3. In the new window, click on download list


How do I logout?

On the top-right of the screen, click on the logout button
